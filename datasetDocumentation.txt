niceDayDataset:
Using google images and the chrome extension "fatkun", a batch image downloader, here is a list of images determined to be best representative of a clear day.
In the dataset, there are roughly 65 images of pure sky, no other obtrusions. 

badDayDataset:
There are 47 images in the training and 5 images in the testing
